Chicken Tikka Masala

About Meal

Chicken tikka masala is a dish consisting of roasted marinated chicken chunks (chicken tikka) in a spiced sauce. The sauce is usually creamy and orange-coloured.

Ingredients:

üç•500g (about 1 pound) boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
üç•1 cup plain yogurt
üç•2 tablespoons ginger garlic paste
üç•2 tablespoons garam masala
üç•1 tablespoon paprika
üç•2 cups tomatoes, diced or 1 can (14 oz) tomato puree
üç•2 medium onions, finely chopped
üç•1/2 cup heavy cream or coconut cream
üç•2 tablespoons butter or oil
üç•Juice of 1 lemon
üç•Salt to taste
üç•Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish (optional)
Optional spices for the marinade:
üç•1 teaspoon turmeric
üç•1 teaspoon chili powder
üç•1 teaspoon coriander powder
üç•1 teaspoon fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi)

Instructions:


üç•Marinate chicken in a mixture of yogurt, ginger garlic paste, garam masala, paprika, lemon juice, and salt for at least 1 hour.
üç•Saut√© finely chopped onions in butter until soft, then add diced tomatoes or tomato puree and cook until thickened.
üç•Stir in heavy cream or coconut cream, simmer for 5-10 minutes to thicken the sauce, season with salt.
üç•Grill or broil marinated chicken until cooked through and slightly charred.
üç•Alternatively, bake chicken in a preheated oven at 400¬∞F for 20-25 minutes.
üç•Remove chicken from skewers and add to the prepared sauce, simmer for an additional 5 minutes.
üç•Adjust seasoning as needed, adding more salt or spices to taste.
üç•Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves if desired.
üç•Serve hot with rice, naan, or roti.
üç•Enjoy your flavorful Chicken Tikka Masala!

Ingredients:


üç•Basmati Rice: 2 cups

üç•Onions: 2 large, thinly sliced
üç•Tomatoes: 2 medium-sized, chopped
üç•Yogurt: 1/2 cup
üç•Ginger Garlic Paste: 2 tablespoons
üç•Green Chilies: 2, slit lengthwise (adjust according to spice preference)
üç•Biryani Masala: 2 tablespoons (store-bought or homemade blend of spices like cumin, coriander, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, etc.)

üç•Saffron Strands: a pinch (optional)
üç•Ghee or Oil: 4 tablespoons
üç•Cilantro (Coriander) Leaves: a handful, chopped

üç•Salt: to taste
üç•Fried Onions: for garnish (optional)
üç•Cashews and Raisins: for garnish (optional)

üç•Saut√© chopped vegetables until partially cooked, then set aside.
üç•In a large pot, heat ghee/oil, saut√© onions until golden, add ginger garlic paste, green chilies, and chopped tomatoes, cook until soft.

Palak paneer

About Meal

Palak Paneer is a popular North Indian dish made with spinach (palak) and paneer (Indian cottage cheese), simmered together in a flavorful sauce.

Ingredients:


üç•Spinach: 500 grams (about 1 pound), fresh or frozen
üç•Paneer: 200-250 grams (about 7-9 ounces), cubed or crumbled
üç•Onions: 2 medium-sized, finely chopped
üç•Tomatoes: 2 medium-sized, chopped
üç•Ginger: 1-inch piece, finely chopped or grated
üç•Garlic: 4-5 cloves, minced
üç•Green Chilies: 2-3, chopped (adjust to taste)
üç•Cumin Seeds: 1 teaspoon
üç•Coriander Powder: 1 teaspoon
üç•Turmeric Powder: 1/2 teaspoon
üç•Garam Masala: 1 teaspoon
üç•Salt: to taste
üç•Oil or Ghee: 2-3 tablespoons
üç•Cream: 1/4 cup (optional, for added richness)
üç•Water or Vegetable Broth: as needed for consistency

Instructions:


üç•Blanch spinach in boiling water for 2 minutes, then transfer to ice water, drain, and blend into a smooth puree.
üç•Heat oil or ghee in a pan, add cumin seeds, chopped onions, ginger, garlic, and green chilies, saut√© until onions turn translucent.
üç•Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
üç•Stir in coriander powder, turmeric powder, and salt, cook for a minute.
üç•Pour in the spinach puree and cook for 5-7 minutes until the raw flavor diminishes.
üç•Add paneer cubes or crumbles to the spinach gravy and simmer for another 5 minutes.
üç•Optionally, add cream for richness and garam masala for extra flavor, stir well.
üç•Serve hot with rice or naan bread. Enjoy your delicious Palak Paneer!

Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a popular Indian dish known for its rich, creamy tomato-based sauce and tender pieces of chicken.

üç•Chicken: 500 grams (about 1 pound), boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
üç•Butter: 3-4 tablespoons
üç•Yogurt: 1/2 cup, plain
üç•Ginger Garlic Paste: 2 tablespoons
üç•Tomato Puree: 1 cup, or 2 large tomatoes pureed
üç•Onion: 1 large, finely chopped
üç•Heavy Cream: 1/2 cup
üç•Cashew Paste: 1/4 cup (optional, for extra creaminess)
üç•Garam Masala: 1 tablespoon
üç•Cumin Powder: 1 teaspoon
üç•Coriander Powder: 1 teaspoon
üç•Chili Powder: 1 teaspoon (adjust to taste)
üç•Turmeric Powder: 1/2 teaspoon
üç•Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves): 1 tablespoon, crushed (optional, for flavor)
üç•Salt: to taste
üç•Sugar: 1 teaspoon (optional, to balance acidity)
üç•Lemon Juice: 1 tablespoon
üç•Fresh Coriander Leaves: for garnish

Instructions:


üç•Marinate chicken pieces in yogurt, ginger garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric, and salt for at least 30 minutes.
üç•Heat butter in a pan, add chopped onions, and saut√© until golden brown.
üç•Add ginger garlic paste and cook until fragrant, then pour in tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
üç•Stir in garam masala, cumin powder, coriander powder, and kasuri methi, cook for a minute.
üç•Add marinated chicken pieces and cook until they are almost cooked through.
üç•Pour in heavy cream and cashew paste (if using), stir well and simmer for another 5-7 minutes.
üç•Adjust seasoning with salt, sugar, and lemon juice according to taste.
üç•Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or naan bread. Enjoy your creamy Butter Chicken!

Sabut Masoor Dal

About Meal

Sabut Masoor Dal is a traditional Indian dish made from whole brown lentils (also known as brown lentils or whole masoor dal).

Ingredients:


üç•Whole Brown Lentils (Sabut Masoor Dal): 1 cup
üç•Onion: 1 large, finely chopped
üç•Tomato: 1 medium-sized, chopped
üç•Garlic: 3-4 cloves, minced
üç•Ginger: 1-inch piece, grated or finely chopped
üç•Green Chilies: 2-3, slit lengthwise (adjust to taste)
üç•Cumin Seeds: 1 teaspoon
üç•Turmeric Powder: 1/2 teaspoon
üç•Coriander Powder: 1 teaspoon
üç•Garam Masala: 1/2 teaspoon
üç•Salt: to taste
üç•Oil or Ghee: 2-3 tablespoons
üç•Water: 3-4 cups (for cooking lentils)
üç•Fresh Cilantro Leaves: a handful, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:


üç•Rinse the whole brown lentils (sabut masoor dal) under running water and soak them for a few hours or overnight.
üç•In a pressure cooker or pot, heat oil or ghee and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.

üç•Once cooked, mash the lentils slightly with the back of a spoon to thicken the consistency.
üç•Garnish with chopped cilantro leaves and serve hot with rice or roti. Enjoy your wholesome Sabut Masoor Dal!

Dal Makhani

About Meal

Dal Makhani is a rich and creamy Indian dish made from black lentils (urad dal) and kidney beans (rajma), simmered with tomatoes, onions, and a blend of aromatic spices.

Ingredients:


üç•Black Lentils (Whole Urad Dal): 1 cup
üç•Kidney Beans (Rajma): 1/4 cup
üç•Onion: 1 large, finely chopped
üç•Tomato: 2 medium-sized, finely chopped or pureed
üç•Ginger: 1-inch piece, grated or finely chopped
üç•Garlic: 4-5 cloves, minced
üç•Green Chilies: 2-3, slit lengthwise (adjust to taste)
üç•Butter: 2-3 tablespoons
üç•Cream: 1/4 cup (optional, for richness)
üç•Cumin Seeds: 1 teaspoon
üç•Coriander Powder: 1 teaspoon
üç•Turmeric Powder: 1/2 teaspoon
üç•Garam Masala: 1 teaspoon
üç•Red Chili Powder: 1/2 teaspoon (adjust to taste)
üç•Salt: to taste
üç•Water: 3-4 cups
üç•Fresh Coriander Leaves: a handful, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:


üç•Rinse black lentils (whole urad dal) and kidney beans (rajma) and soak them separately overnight or for at least 8 hours.
üç•Pressure cook the soaked lentils and kidney beans with water until soft and mushy, then set aside.
üç•In a pan, heat butter and add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add chopped onions, ginger, garlic, and green chilies, and saut√© until golden brown.
üç•Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy.
üç•Stir in coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt, and cook for a couple of minutes.
üç•Add the cooked lentils and kidney beans along with some water, and simmer for about 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally.
üç•Once the dal has thickened and flavors have melded together, add garam masala and cream (if using), and cook for another 5 minutes.
üç•Adjust seasoning if needed, then garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
üç•Serve hot with rice or naan bread. Enjoy your creamy and flavorful Dal Makhani!

üç•Oil: for deep frying
